ESAR is a unique business that offers a variety of services, combining the functions of an NGO. Established in 1989, ESAR's main objective is to provide services to the people of Afghanistan, both within the country and in refugee camps in Pakhtonkhaw. As an NGO, ESAR is dedicated to making a positive impact on society and has a strong track record of collaboration with national and international organizations, donors, and government authorities at the local and national levels. They specialize in consultancy services, WASH construction projects, providing high-quality services in virtical and horizintal infrastructure development. Our Engineering Survices


Surveying is a basic element of all civil engineering projects. Surveying is “the science of making precise measurements between known and unknown points, applying the principals of mathematics to determine positional locations at a required accuracy, and the art of publishing mapping products which meet the needs of the customer”. Surveying determines the relative spatial locations of points or objects (features) on or near the surface of the earth, for the purpose of graphically portraying the terrain data or geometrically locating their 3D coordinates. In transportation engineering; surveying provides the foundation and continuity for route location, land appropriation, design and construction of capital projects. Surveys also establish a basic "framework" of horizontal and vertical control which is valuable in the stakeout of engineered designs during the construction of a project, as well as in the reestablishment of highway right of way.
